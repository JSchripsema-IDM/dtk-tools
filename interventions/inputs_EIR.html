Inputs EIR¶

dtk.interventions.input_EIR.add_InputEIR(cb, monthlyEIRs, age_dependence='SURFACE_AREA_DEPENDENT', start_day=0)[source]¶

Create an intervention introducing new infections (see InputEIR for detail)

Parameters:
  • cb – The DTKConfigBuilder containing the campaign parameters
  • monthlyEIRs – a list of monthly EIRs (must be 12 items)
  • age_dependence – “LINEAR” or “SURFACE_AREA_DEPENDENT”
  • start_day – Start day of the introduction of new infections
Returns:

Nothing
