Habitat scale campaign¶

Represents a campaign scaling down the larval habitat.

dtk.interventions.habitat_scale.scale_larval_habitats(cb, scales, target={'ALL_HABITATS': 1}, variation=0, start_day=0)[source]¶

Reduce available larval habitat in a node-specific way.

Parameters
  • cb -- The DTKConfigBuilder object
  • scales -- Dictionary associating a nodeID with the larval habitat scale
  • target -- the target habitat affected by the reduction
  • variation -- Faction of the overall scale used when randomizing the scale
Returns

Nothing
